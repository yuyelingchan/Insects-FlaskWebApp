British Insects

The insects in the UK which we are interested in are:

1. Cicada

The only cicada species in the UK is New Forest Cicada. It is a species in Cicadidae Family.

2. Bush Cricket

Insects in the family Tettigoniidae are known as bush crickets (also called katydids).
The species in this family which we have collected sound samples are:

  • Subfamily Phaneropterinae:

    • [ Sickle Bearing Bush Cricket ] and [ Speckled Bush Cricket ]

  • Subfamily Tettigoniidae:

    • [ Great Green Bush Cricket ]

  • Subfamily Conocephalinae:

    • [ Short Winged Conehead ] and [ Long Winged Conehead ]

  • Subfamily Decticinae:

    • [ Dark Bush Cricket ], [ Wartbiter ], [ Grey Bush Cricket ], [ Bog Bush Cricket ] and [ Roesel Bush Cricket ]


    3. Cricket

    Family Gryllidae, known as Crickets.
    We collected sound samples of the following species:

  • Subfamily Gryllinae:

    • [ Filed Cricket ] and [ House Cricket ]

  • Subfamily Nemobiinae:

    • [ Wood Cricket ] and [ Tree Cricket ]


    4. Grasshopper

    Grasshoppers are the insects from insect family Acrididae.
    Main species in the UK are:

  • Subfamily Gomphocerinae:

    • [ Field Grasshopper ], [ Lesser Marsh Grasshopper ], [ Meadow Grasshopper ], [ Motteled Grasshopper ], [ Lesser Motteled Grasshopper ], [ Common Green Grasshopper ], [Woodland Grasshopper ], [ Heath Grasshopper ], [ Rufous Grasshopper ]

  • Subfamily Oedipodinae:

    • [ Large Marsh Grasshopper ] and [ Strip Winged Grasshopper ]


    5. Mole Cricket

    Mole Cricket is the only species in the family Gryllotalpidae.
